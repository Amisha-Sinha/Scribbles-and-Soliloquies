Our Story

Weaving words onto the tapestry of Wisdom

Scribbles & Soliloquies is a collection of all my personal compositions. It was incepted with the idea of creating a collection of all my written works. I have always wanted to consolidate all my work into a singlular location which I could both, cherish and celebrate.

The joy of reading multiplies when shared. Hence, Scribbles & Soliloquies is not just a personal treasure but also a platform for channelizing my personal thoughts through literary discourse because I firmly believe that success in any writing lies in the emotions and thoughts that it is able to evoke from its readers.
With this goal in sight,
Welcome aboard Scribbles & Soliloquies!

Happy Reading!

ABOUT MYSELF

Amisha Sinha

Greetings!

I am Amisha Sinha, the creator and sole author behind Scribbles & Soliloquies. Describing myself as a quietly observant soul amidst the hustle of everyday life, I attribute my passion for writing to the seeds of curiosity sown in my adolescence.
With an ardent love for literature, I find joy in crafting narratives that underline the subtle emotions often overlooked amidst life's clamor.

Through my stories, I endeavor not only to impart wisdom but also to provoke introspection. By establishing this platform, my aim is to curate a cherished collection of my work and foster a space for sharing thoughts, theories, and sentiments through diverse narratives. I earnestly hope that you appreciate the work.
It would mean the world to me!
